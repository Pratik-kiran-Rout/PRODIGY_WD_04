About Me

Hello! I'm Pratik Kiran Rout, a passionate web developer with a strong foundation in both frontend and backend technologies. My journey in web development began with a curiosity about how websites work, which led me to dive deep into coding and software development.

I have hands-on experience in coding through personal and academic projects, building a strong foundation in both frontend and backend development.
